Basestack Feature Flags is a self-hosting platform that provides an effortless method to host your very own Feature Flags management platform. By leveraging services like [Vercel ↗](https://vercel.com/?ref=basestack.co), any developer, whether an indie or startup, gains full control over their features and data. Ultimately, the platform is housed in your account, under your management, giving you complete control over your data.

Quickly self-host the Feature Flags Platform on your desired infrastructure. Choose one of the available official guides below, with more coming soon.

### Fork the Platform

Alternatively, you have the option to manually fork the [Basestack Platform repository](https://github.com/basestack-co/basestack). This provides greater control over [updates](/docs/self-hosting/updates) and even allows you to [contribute](/docs/self-hosting/contributing) to the project if desired.
